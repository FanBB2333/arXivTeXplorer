# Privacy Policy for arXivTeXplorer

**Last Updated:** December 12, 2025

arXivTeXplorer ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
We do **not** collect, store, or share any of your personal identification information or browsing history on our servers. The extension runs entirely within your browser.

- **arXiv Content**: The extension fetches public paper data and source files directly from `arxiv.org` and `export.arxiv.org` purely for the purpose of displaying them to you.
- **User Preferences**: We use the Chrome Storage API (`chrome.storage`) to save your local settings (such as editor configuration) to improve your user experience. This data stays on your device (or is synced via your Chrome account if enabled) and is not accessible to us.

## 2. Third-Party Services
The extension interacts with the following third-party service:
- **arXiv.org**: To retrieve paper metadata and source files. Please refer to [arXiv's Privacy Policy](https://arxiv.org/help/policies/privacy_policy) for information on how they handle your data when you access their content.

## 3. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
